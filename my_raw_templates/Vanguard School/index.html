Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to important questions regarding admission procedures, board affiliation, transport, and classroom ratios.

Vanguard International School is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), New Delhi. We deliver a comprehensive, globally-aligned curriculum focused on academic mastery, analytical thinking, and co-curricular integration.

For Nursery admissions, the child must be at least 3 years of age as of June 1st of the academic year. For LKG admissions, the child must be at least 4 years of age as of June 1st. Standard documentation (Birth Certificate, Address Proof, Transfer Certificate, and Immunization Records) must be provided during enrolment.

Yes, we operate an extensive fleet of air-conditioned, GPS-tracked school buses covering major routes in Bengaluru. Each bus is staffed with a dedicated driver, a security assistant, and a female attendant, and is monitored through central dashboard systems for safety.

We maintain an average student-teacher ratio of 1:15 in Pre-Primary classes (Nursery to UKG) with support assistants, and 1:24 in Primary, Middle, and High School classes to guarantee individualized attention and high classroom quality.

Vanguard features a professional sports complex (turf football ground, indoor swimming academy, tennis courts, cricket pitches) alongside a modern Performing Arts wing offering structured classes in contemporary music, sculpting, robotics, coding, debate, and drama.
