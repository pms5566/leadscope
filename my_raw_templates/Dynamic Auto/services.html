Car Detailing Services

We offer comprehensive exterior and interior styling protection options for your vehicle. Explore our professional-grade detailing options below.

Protection

Ceramic Coating

Our professional-grade Ceramic Coatings form a semi-permanent covalent bond with your vehicle's clear coat. This creating an ultra-slick, high-gloss hydrophobic layer that repels water, road grime, mud, and contaminants, making it exceptionally easy to wash. Provides long-term protection against UV rays, bird droppings, acid rain, and environmental oxidation.

  • Multi-year durability
  • High-Gloss shine
  • Intense hydrophobics
  • Chemical protection
  • Hard protection layer
  • Easy maintenance

Refine & Shine

Exterior Detailing

Restore your car’s exterior body panels. Finished with a premium wax or paint sealant to provide a glossy layer of paint protection.

Clarity & Correction

Paint Correction

Eliminate ugly swirl marks, spiderweb scratches, hologram reflections, bird poop etching, and oxidation. We measure paint thickness and perform a precise multi-stage machine polishing correction using high-grade compounds and pads to safely restore maximum optical clarity, depth, and reflection in your car’s clear coat.

  • Swirl mark removal
  • Scratch leveling
  • Multi-stage machine polish
  • Paint thickness check
  • Gloss level optimization
  • Prep for Ceramic Coating
